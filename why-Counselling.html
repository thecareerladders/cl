Need for Counselling

One important takeaway from Anshu’s experience so far is that we need to "Catch Them Young" meaning, students should be counselled from a young age as the earlier we start, we can plan in a systematic, focused manner so that right from childhood, holistic development of the personality can be targeted. She is of the opinion that information and guidance pertaining to the career at the right age is a boon. Through her digital platform, she aims to reach out to maximum number of students across India and outside India. She wants to gradually expand her services to the rural areas as she believes there is a lot of untapped potential which is not getting leveraged due to lack of guidance and proper mentoring.
