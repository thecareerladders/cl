About Anshu Tiwari

Career Guidance

Need for Counselling

A counsellor is one of the key persons in shaping the career of a student. Hence, it demands thorough research and continuous upgradation on the part of any counsellor. Anshu believes that “A Plan without a Goal is just a wish”, so she helps the students in identifying their respective goals and aligning the talents and skills of these students along with those goals. Once the goals are clear, students along with the parents just have to do their bit by taking the required steps towards those goals in a definite time frame. A counsellor can lay out the entire road map to achieve these goals in the defined time frame by leveraging his or her experience.
